Call for Speakers
=================

Click here to send us an abstract, a short description and the title of your
proposed talk. Your talk should be a maximum of 55 minutes. Try to structure
your talk around our theme, "Work and Play with Python".

Please indicate if you are fine with being filmed for our official Python.ph
video repository.

We will be contacting you before June, a month before PyCon 2015 starts.
